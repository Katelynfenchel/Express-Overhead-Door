Openers

We provide service and repair of electric garage door openers. Although we work with most major brands, Liftmaster openers are our opener of choice. Creative Garage Doors offer a range of services for garage door openers including inspection, repair, adjustment, and lubrication if needed. We can also inspect the mounting of the unit as well as its attachment to the door itself. We carry a wide variety of parts and are able to service and repair most major garage door opener brands.

Springs

There are extension springs and torsion springs.An extension spring needs to be replaced with a torsion system. Extension springs are extremely dangerous and can cause death or damage to you, your house or your car. Extension springs can fly across the room when they break. The torsion spring system is not as dangerous. It has springs wrapped around a tube at the top of your door. On average these last 6 to 10 years, depending on usage. High cycle springs last 15 to 30 years and are available upon request.
